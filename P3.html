<h1><font color="red">Chocolate</font> Chip Cookies Recipe EP1 - <font color="red">Peter</font> Cooks!</h1>



<h2>Ingredients:</h2>

<ul>

<li>1 cup butter</li>

<li>1 cup sugar</li>

<li>2 cups flour</li>

<li>1 tsp vanilla extract</li>

<li>1 cup chocolate chips</li>

</ul>



<h2>Instructions:</h2>

<ol>

<li>Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).</li>

<li>Cream together the butter and sugar in a bowl.</li>

<li>Add flour and mix well.</li>

<li>Stir in the chocolate chips.</li>

<li>Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.</li>

</ol>
